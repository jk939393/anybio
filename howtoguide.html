Quick Guide: Using the bioRxiv Plugin with ChatGPT

Note: The plugin is activated by the keywords "research" and "paper".

Fetching Papers:

To fetch a paper, simply type your request in the ChatGPT interface. Examples:

  • "Get a research paper on molecules from bioRxiv."
  • "Find me a bioRxiv paper on sharks."
  • "Show nuclear fusion research papers from bioRxiv after 2022."

Navigating Results:

ChatGPT will display a list of relevant papers based on your query.

  • Click on any title to view more details.
  • If you wish to see more results or navigate to a different page of results, use commands like:
    • "Show me page 3 of the results."
    • "Get to the next page."
